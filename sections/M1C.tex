  Mittelwert &\ $U_{R AV} = \frac{1}{2\pi}\int\limits_{\alpha}^{\pi}U_{2m} \cdot sin(\beta) \cdot d\beta, \beta = \omega t$\\

  allgemein &\ $\int\limits_{\alpha}^{\pi}sin^2(\beta) \cdot d\beta = \frac{\pi-\alpha}{2}+\frac{sin(2\alpha)}{4}$\\\\
